FAQS

General Questions

❓ What is Advanced Finance Tracker?

✅ Advanced Finance Tracker is an AI-powered personal finance management system that helps users track expenses, set budgets, manage investments, detect fraud, and receive smart financial insights.

❓ Is Advanced Finance Tracker free to use?

✅ We offer both free and premium plans. The free plan includes basic expense tracking, budgeting, and AI insights, while the premium plan unlocks advanced features like investment tracking, fraud detection, and personalized financial planning.

❓ Which platforms is Advanced Finance Tracker available on?

✅ You can access our finance tracker via a web app, Android app, and iOS app, ensuring seamless financial tracking across all your devices.

Account & Security

❓ How do I create an account?

✅ Simply click Sign Up, enter your email, set a password, and verify your account via OTP. You can also sign up using Google or Apple ID.

❓ How secure is my financial data?

✅ Security is our top priority. We use bank-grade encryption, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and fraud detection to ensure your data remains private and protected.

❓ What should I do if I forget my password?

✅ Click on Forgot Password?, enter your registered email, and follow the instructions to reset your password securely.

❓ Can I delete my account?

✅ Yes, you can request account deletion under Settings > Account Management or contact support@finance-tracker.com for assistance.
