Privacy Policy

  • 📌 Personal Data: Name, Email, Contact Info.

How We Use Your Data

  • ✅ To track expenses and provide AI-driven insights.
  • ✅ To ensure security and prevent fraud.
  • ✅ To personalize user experience and financial planning.

Data Protection & Security

We use bank-grade encryption and advanced security measures to keep your data safe.

  • 🔐 AES-256 encryption for financial transactions.
  • 🛡️ Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for added security.
  • 🚨 AI-based fraud detection and suspicious activity alerts.

Third-Party Integrations

We integrate with trusted services to enhance functionality while ensuring privacy compliance.

  • 🔹 Razorpay & Stripe - Secure Payments.

Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your data and can:

  • 🗑️ Account deletion permanently removes all associated data within 30 days.
